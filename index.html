How to Play Simon Game

  1. Start the Game:

    • press any key from keyboard to start the game.

  2. Watch and Remember:

    • Pay attention to the sequence of colors and sounds that light up.

  3. Repeat the Sequence:

    • After the sequence finishes, click or press the colors in the same order as they appeared.

  4. Advance Levels:

    • As you correctly repeat the sequences, the game will add more colors, making it progressively challenging.

Remember, the goal is to see how far you can go without making a mistake! Good luck!
